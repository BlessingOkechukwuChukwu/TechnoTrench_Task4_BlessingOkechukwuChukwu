The email and login page you interacted with were part of a security awareness exercise. No personal information was collected.

Phishing attacks are becoming increasingly sophisticated. Here are some tips to help you identify and avoid real phishing attempts:

  • Always verify the sender's email address carefully.
  • Be cautious of urgent or threatening language in emails.
  • Hover over links before clicking to see the true destination.
  • Never provide sensitive information through email or on unfamiliar websites.
  • When in doubt, contact the IT department directly using known contact information.

Remember, staying vigilant is our best defense against cyber threats. If you suspect a real phishing attempt, report it to IT security immediately.
